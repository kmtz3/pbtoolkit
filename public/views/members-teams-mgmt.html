Members & Teams

View and manage team membership in real time.
How to use
  • Edit team details — click a team name, handle, or description to edit inline. Press Enter to save, Escape to cancel.
  • Move a member — drag a member row and drop it onto another team card.
  • Copy a member — hold Shift while dragging to add them to the target team without removing from the original.
  • Remove a member — click the × button on a member row.
  • Add a member — click "+ Add member" on a team card and search by name or email.
  • Create / delete teams — use the "+ New team" button or the "Delete team" link on each card.
  • Filter — type in the search bar to filter teams and members by name, handle, or email.
