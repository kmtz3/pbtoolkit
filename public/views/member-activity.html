Export member activity
Export activity data enriched with member profiles and team assignments. Use for license auditing and enablement planning.
How to use
  • Date range — pick a preset or enter custom dates to scope the export period.
  • Filters — narrow by role, team, and active/inactive status. Use "Include members with no records" for a full roster.
  • Raw mode — check "Export raw daily data" for one row per member per day instead of totals.
  • Export — click "Export activity" to generate the CSV. Use "Stop" to cancel mid-export.
⚠️ Member names and emails are obfuscated. Your token may lack PII access.
